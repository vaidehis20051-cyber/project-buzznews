About Us

At BuzzNews, we are committed to delivering accurate, trustworthy, and inclusive news to our readers. Our editorial values are guided by transparency, fairness, and integrity. We believe in giving a voice to local communities through our unique Local Voices feature, which allows individuals across Gujarat to share firsthand updates, stories, and events from their districts.

We uphold the principles of impartiality and responsibility, ensuring that every news piece is fact-checked and presented with context. Our mission is to empower readers with knowledge and build a platform where communities are heard and represented authentically.
